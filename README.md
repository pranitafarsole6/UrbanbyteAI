# 🌱 UrbanByte AI  
### Intelligent Digital Waste Management for Sustainable Cloud Usage

---

## 📌 Overview

UrbanByte AI is an intelligent system designed to **reduce digital waste and its environmental impact**.  
It analyzes user storage, identifies unnecessary data, and provides actionable insights to optimize storage and reduce carbon emissions.

👉 Our goal is to connect **digital behavior with environmental sustainability**.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

- Rapid growth of digital data leads to **unnecessary storage**
- Duplicate files, unused media, and redundant backups increase cloud load
- Data centers consume **massive energy (1–1.5% of global electricity)**
- This results in **carbon emissions and environmental degradation**

---

## 💡 Solution

UrbanByte AI provides:
- Smart data scanning  
- AI-based duplicate & similarity detection  
- Storage insights & recommendations  
- Carbon footprint estimation  

👉 Turning digital waste into **measurable environmental impact**

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **Scan** → Extracts metadata from files  
2. **Analyze** → Detects duplicates & unnecessary data  
3. **Identify** → Finds optimization opportunities  
4. **Visualize** → Displays storage insights  
5. **Recommend** → Suggests cleanup actions  

---

## 🌍 Sustainability Impact

- Reduces unnecessary data storage  
- Decreases data center load  
- Saves energy used in storage & cooling  
- Lowers carbon emissions  

👉 Example:  
Deleting small redundant data can reduce CO₂ emissions, which scales significantly across users.

---

## 🔬 Carbon Emission Estimation

We estimate carbon emissions using:

CO₂ (kg) ≈ Data Stored (GB) × 2

This is based on average energy consumption and emission factors for cloud storage.
